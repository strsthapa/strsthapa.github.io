I'm Suraj Thapa, PE — a Structural Project Engineer at Cornerstone Engineering Inc. (Louisville, KY) with 10+ years of experience across Nepal, Thailand, and the USA. My work spans water and wastewater infrastructure, industrial facilities, seismic assessment, and complex retrofit design.

Previously at Earthquake Safety Solutions (ESS) and Miyamoto International Nepal (MIN), I led multi-facility seismic retrofits, post-earthquake assessments, and performance-based designs for hospitals, schools, and high-rise buildings — coordinating with international firms including Arup.

I specialize in nonlinear seismic analysis (Perform-3D, ASCE 41-13), environmental concrete design (ACI 350), and complex foundation systems. As Team Leader on multi-million dollar programs, I bring full project lifecycle management from field assessment to final report delivery.
